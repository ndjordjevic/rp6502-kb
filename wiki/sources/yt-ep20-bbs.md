# yt-ep20 — The 6502 Calls a BBS

**Summary**: Announces the Pi Pico 1 → Pi Pico 2 hardware upgrade path and demonstrates the RIA-W WiFi capabilities: BBS access with ANSI/CP437 art, NTP with DST adjustment.

---

## Key topics

- **Hardware upgrade**: swap Pi Pico 1 boards with Pi Pico 2 — plain Pico 2 for VGA slot, Pico 2 W for RIA slot. Pico 2 W includes WiFi radio.
- **BBS access**: connect to bulletin board systems over the internet. ANSI escape sequences and CP437 fonts already in the Picocomputer stack → BBS art renders correctly.
- **NTP**: fetch current time from internet; handles daylight saving time adjustment automatically.
- **Note**: the transcript is brief (Ep20 is a short announcement-style episode).
